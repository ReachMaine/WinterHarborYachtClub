Winter Harbor Yacht Club

Welcome to the Winter Harbor Yacht Club website created to provide members with the most up-to-date information about your club and its activities. Summers are short and you don't want to miss anything!

Our club is the center of social life on Grindstone Neck, so all club events with dates and times are included on this website. Also the Junior Sailing schedule and the Snack Bar and menus are available.
