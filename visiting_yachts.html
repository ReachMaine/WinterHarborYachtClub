Winter Harbor Yacht Club
Visiting Yachts

Moorings:

Winter Harbor Yacht Club has some visitor moorings. The Club usually monitors Channel 9 and can give direction to mooring availability during the hours of 9 am to 5 pm during the summer season. Check the WHYC Calendar for season dates.

Cost of Moorings per day:

40 feet and under $30
40-55 feet and over $45
55 feet and over $60
Includes showers and 1 bag of trash.
Yachts on own anchor - trash $5 a bag and showers $5 each.

Shower Hours:

9 to 10 AM and 3 to 4 PM

Launch Service:

*The Club Launch is available to visiting yachts when in service. Normal launch hours are: 9 am to 5 pm.

Dock Space

Dock space is for loading and unloading. NO OVERNIGHT DOCKING IS AVAILABLE.


*Please note, at times the launch may not be in service during normal hours. Service cannot be guaranteed.
